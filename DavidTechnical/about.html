About Me

My name is David and my whole life I have grown up with a computer and have always loved the technology and advances they provide. The knowledge base is so huge, it's a never ending process and I hope to get a career in the field I enjoy. I worked with a landlord for a few years and learned how to re-wire and re-plumb entire houses. With the skills I acquired, I bought my own rental properties that I run and manage. A few years after that, I switched jobs to appliance repair and have been repairing appliances ever since. While working, I stayed in college consistently throughout the years and hope to graduate at the end of fall 2016 in the computer science field with my math minor.


For fun, I like to scuba dive and have I swam many of the Florida springs and I have even gone to the Gulf to search for Megalodon shark teeth. In the past year I have been taking on my fear of heights and started climbing rock walls, which also makes for fun exercise. Lastly, I have been riding motorcycles for the past 9 years for fun and it saves a ton of gas for commuting compared to my car.
